Our Roots

Direct Trade Excellence

At Brewora, we believe transparency is as important as taste. We source our beans directly from small-holder farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Brazil. By cutting out the middleman, we ensure farmers receive premium prices while we secure the highest quality 'Specialty Grade' harvests for our roasting process.
